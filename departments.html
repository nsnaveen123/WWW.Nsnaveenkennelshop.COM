Kombai Dog

The Kombai is an Indian dog breed and just like the other Indian breeds, it draws its name after the town in which it was developed i.e. Kombai in Tamil Nadu.

History

This dog breed has been developed in Southern part of India. It is a general belief that this breed and its brown and red coats with black mask has made people believe that it is descend of fox.
